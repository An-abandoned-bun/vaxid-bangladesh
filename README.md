# VaxID Bangladesh

**One child. One secure record. A healthier Bangladesh.**

VaxID Bangladesh is a birth-linked QR child health ID and vaccination intelligence platform for hospitals, clinics, NGOs, and government health programs.

## Features

- Child registration
- Random non-iterative birth certificate number generation
- QR-linked child profile
- Vaccination record tracking
- Patient record management
- AI Review powered by Groq
- Backend routing through Cloudflare
- Firebase database and hosting
- Offline support for clinics and hospitals
- Vaccination Alert SMS system
- Android SIM SMS automation prototype using Termux
- Bangladesh-style printable birth certificate demo

## Business Model

Patients pay nothing. Hospitals, clinics, NGOs, and government health partners pay a monthly software license fee.

## AI Safety

VaxID does not diagnose, prescribe medicine, choose vaccine dose, or replace doctors. It only flags possible doctor-review needs for authorized medical staff.

## Privacy Promise

VaxID will not sell, rent, trade, or share collected child, guardian, vaccination, or patient data with unauthorized third parties.
